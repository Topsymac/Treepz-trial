The Treepz Story

Our story started when Onyeka Akumah, CEO/Co-founder of treepz, commuted via all 3 modes of transportation in 1 day. He took an airplane, a ferry and a public bus. His experience on a public bus in Lagos, Nigeria was an unpleasant one for the books.

It was at that moment he knew that someone had to change the narrative for the better. In collaboration with the brightest minds, Treepz was born to revolutionise the public transportation sector in Africa using technology-driven solutions.

Our Values

These are not just words on a page.

At treepz, our values shape our actions.

Humility

To each one, we accord respect and humility. In achieving success, we can never underestimate the value of each person's views and perspectives.

Hunger

We must be seen as people who are constantly and consistently thinking out of the box and re-inventing themselves. making today's achievements the starting point for our success stories for tomorrow.

Integrity

We not only comply with regulations, good business practices and ethical standards, but we also are honest, reliable, and trustworthy people.

Excellence

Excellence for us, is not just a watchword, it's an intrinsic habit. It's the default mode for all our operational standards so that mediocrity & half measures become alien to our culture.

People Smart

We have Interpersonal intelligence and are constantly seeking to improve our relationships with our customers and our colleagues.

Have Fun

All work and no play will not only make us dull but will dampen our creative talents. Having fun allows us to be flexible and also creates an enabling environment where we can exhale every once in a while.

Speed

Speed is central to everything we are and everything we are and everything we do; its being quick-thinking in creating and implementing innovative solutions, being fast in our time-responsiveness to our clients demand and being at the forefront of ever-changing technology. We live, think and do everything faster,faster, faster !
